About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1982

Kasikeu Girls Secondary School was founded in 1982 by the community of Kasikeu in Makueni County with a vision to provide quality education to girls in the region. Over four decades, we have grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in Makueni County, Kenya.

Our Mission

"To provide a holistic, quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and leadership in young women, preparing them to positively contribute to society."

We believe every girl deserves an education that builds resilience, critical thinking, confidence, and a deep sense of purpose. Our teachers, facilities, and programs are designed with this belief at their core.

Admissions Open — Form 1, 2026

We welcome applications from bright, motivated young women who wish to pursue academic excellence in a supportive, disciplined, and inspiring environment.

Application Process
1

Collect Application Form

Download the admission form from our website or collect a physical copy from the school office. Forms are available from January each year.

2

Complete & Submit Documents

Fill in the form completely and attach all required documents including KCPE result slip, birth certificate, and 2 passport photographs.

3

Selection & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a brief interview and assessment. Selection is merit-based and results announced in February.

4

Admission & Fee Payment

Successful candidates receive an admission letter and must confirm their place by paying the required fees and purchasing uniforms and books.

5

Welcome to Kasikeu Girls!

Report on the designated date with all required items. Your journey to excellence begins here — karibu sana!

Admission Requirements

  • KCPE Certificate (min. 280 marks recommended)
  • Birth Certificate (original & photocopy)
  • NEMIS Learner Number
  • 2 Recent Passport Photographs
  • Primary School Leaving Certificate
  • Medical Certificate of Health
  • Completed Application Form
