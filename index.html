Admissions Open — Form 1, 2025

We welcome bright, motivated young women.

Application Process
1

Collect Application Form

Download the admission form from our website or collect from the school office. Forms available from January.

2

Complete & Submit Documents

Fill the form and attach required documents including KCPE result slip, birth certificate, and passport photos.

3

Selection & Interview

Shortlisted candidates are invited for assessment. Selection is merit-based; results announced in February.

4

Admission & Fee Payment

Successful candidates receive an admission letter and must confirm by paying fees and purchasing uniforms.

5

Welcome to Kasikeu Girls!

Report on the designated date with all requirements. Your journey to excellence begins here!

Admission Requirements

  • KCPE Certificate (min. 280 marks recommended)
  • Birth Certificate (original & copy)
  • NEMIS Learner Number
  • 2 Recent Passport Photographs
  • Primary School Leaving Certificate
  • Medical Certificate of Health
  • Completed Application Form
